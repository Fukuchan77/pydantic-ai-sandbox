## Deviations from Design

- **BLE001 noqa premise corrected.** Tasks 2.3/7.2 mandated a `# noqa: BLE001`
  on the broad except, but ruff's BLE001 flags only *swallowing* excepts; the
  litellm except **re-raises** (`raise … from exc`), so the noqa was intrinsically
  unused (`RUF100`). Resolution (user-approved): enabled `BLE` in ruff `select`,
  the re-raising except keeps a plain rationale comment (no noqa), and the
  genuinely swallowing `logging_setup.py:154` catch carries the noqa. Spec wording
  amended to match shipped reality.
- **`num_retries=0` live assertion down-scoped (user-approved "正直にダウンスコープ").**
  The planned per-request upstream-POST-count equality proved doubly flawed at
  live time: (1) LiteLLM issues the watsonx inference over its own *aiohttp*
  transport, invisible to `instrument_httpx` (only the IAM-token httpx POST shows);
  (2) a happy-path request reveals no retry budget regardless of `num_retries`.
  Removed the unprovable assertion; kept env-routing/observability/response
  assertions (all live-green). Deferred a genuine suppression proof to a
  forced-failure lane.
- **Major 4 landed as one cohesive unit (4.1+4.2+4.3+5.1+5.2).** The builder
  rewrite breaks the old `OpenAIChatModel` construction test, and its faithful
  replacement (5.1) asserts the 4.3 env reconciliation + 4.2 guard — so a green
  gate was inseparable from the whole coupled change. User-approved scope.
